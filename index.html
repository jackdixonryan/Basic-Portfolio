About Me

I am Jack Ryan. I am a writer, a traveller, a lifelong learner and, with any luck, a soon-to-be full stack web designer. I earned my BA at Kenyon College in rural Ohio, where I studied English with an emphasis on fiction writing, and minored in the Arabic language.

I am currently an associate at Amazon Fresh in North Denver, a facility that delivers fresh groceries throughout the city of Denver. I work in a hectic environment, which brings daily challenges and lessons in unpredictability.

When I am neither working nor writing, I'm often travelling. The photo to the left was taken in Iceland, at the base of a waterfall which has an inscrutable name that I cannot remember. I found a tremendous deal of inspiration on our family's long ring road trek around the island, where we saw everything from volcanic craters to growing iceberg lagoons.

My hobbies include skiing, hiking, and brewing OK (though admittedly never top quality) beer with my cousin from our ever-increasing line of craft brews, Basement Brewing. So far, our black IPA "Heart of Darkness" has been the pinnacle of our achievements.
